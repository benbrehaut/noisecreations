about noisecreations

A little bit about us

noisecreations was co-founded by Callum Tennick and Philip Moroz in the summer of 2013 in Southampton, UK. Below is a brief bio of their respective backgrounds.

Follow noisecreations to gain updates on our progress and discounts!

Callum Tennick

Callum has a keen interest in producing sound effects ever since working on audio replacement projects at college. Since then he has been working on a variety of projects with film-makers and game developers.

By getting involved in projects for various types of media, Callum has expanded his knowledge on recording, editing, file management, and metadata to ensure that quality work is produced.

With his experience ever evolving, noisecreations showcases his recording and editing skills in a collection that you can use in your projects.

Philip Moroz

Philip's devotion to sound editing and field recording developed from his background in music performance and music recording. A natural transition into an interest in film sound began during his time in college. The interest soon became a devotion.

Producing noisecreations as a collective project during university still proves to be rewarding - experimenting with sound in spare time and producing a range of collections to showcase their ideas.

Philip currently works full-time work as an Audio Assistant at Hackenbacker Audio Post-Production in Soho, London.
