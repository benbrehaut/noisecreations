About noisecreations

we create high quality, affordable sound effect collections

A little bit about us!

noisecreations was co-founded by Callum Tennick and Philip Moroz in the summer of 2013 in Southampton, UK.

Its aim is to provide high end sound effects collections that focus on sounds that are difficult or time-consuming to acquire. Since then noisecreations has expanded to provide post-production services from foley, sound design to the final mix.

It is early days for noisecreations although the pace is building exponentially. We have more awesome sound effects collections in the pipeline which we are very excited to release ..so watch this space.

Callum Tennick

Callum started his adventure in sound recording in 2007, when he was able to use his schools recording equipment to record himself playing the guitar.

Now he works on various productions in various positions, from recording audio on location. to editing and sound design.

Callum is very interested in the editing side of audio production, and hopes to use his recording and editing skills to provide the best quality sound effect collections.

Philip Moroz

Philip is an aspiring sound designer with an ambition to bring a whole new edge to the sound of film.

Sound has been an important part of Philip's life from a young age, playing musical instruments to recording location audio for small productions with friends.

From a musical background, he has always been interested in new ways of producing sound for picture and so channels these ambitions into capturing sounds that are interesting, time-consuming and/or difficult to record.
