Projector Sound Collection

365 sounds, 1.91GB, 24bit/96 kHz

noisecreations unleashes PROJECTOR. From modern digital projection bleeps and extraction fan sounds to the iconic intermittent sprocket ticks from classic film projectors, this collection captures a huge variety of sounds in stunning quality to enhance your next project.
