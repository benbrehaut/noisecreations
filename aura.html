noisecreations' fourth sound effects collection - AURA. This royalty-free collection provides you with high-quality ambiences from an extensive array of locations around the UK. We designed this collection to be as versatile and as resourceful as possible to be used across a broad range of applications such as film, television, games, theatre and so on. Therefore we recorded a plethora of rural and urban ambiences from pleasant birdsong and woodland to roaring trains and high street hustle and bustle.

This collection contains ambiences such as shopping centres, supermarkets, cafes, car parks, cemeteries, forests, gyms, highway traffic, bars, pubs, libraries, city backstreets, parks, London underground trains, over ground trains (interior and exterior), hallways, neighbourhoods, iconic British train stations, busy junctions, high streets, swimming pools, hustling offices and several windy environments.
